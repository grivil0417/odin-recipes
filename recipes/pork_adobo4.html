Pork Adobo | A Filipino Classic

Classic chicken adobo recipe that's simple to make and loved by all who try it. It has been modified to be a bit saucier than traditional adobo and is delicious served over rice.

Ingredients

  • One (1) kilogram of chicken
  • One (1) head of garlic
  • One (1) small onion
  • 250 ml Sprite
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 3/4 cup vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • Pepper Corns and optional chilis to taste
